Abstract Submission Guidelines

Please follow the guidelines below for submitting your abstract for poster oral presentation to the email ID:

Email: icasb2025@gmail.com

Download the Abstract Submission Form here: Download PDF

  1. The abstract should be written in English and not exceed 250 words.
  2. Use Times New Roman font, size 12, and maintain single line spacing.
  3. Kindly underline the name of the presenting author and provide the details of the corresponding author.
  4. The poster should be in portrait mode with dimensions of 3.5 in length and 3 in width.
  5. The title and author names should be displayed at the top of the poster..
  6. Send the abstract as an email attachment with the subject line: "Poster Oral Abstract Submission".
  7. I am confident that the given guidelines will be followed diligently.
