About the Conference

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya (A Central University) is delighted to welcome you to the International Conference on Systems Biology (ICASB-25) at Dr. Harisingh Gour Central University, Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, India, from 6 to 8th March 2025. This event will bring together leading scientists, academicians, industry professionals, speakers, and experts on one platform. The informative discussions will highlight trending issues, solutions, achievements, and future strategies in the field of Systems Biology.

Conference Objectives

Latest Techniques in Systems Biology

The conference aims to present the latest techniques, methodologies, and skills in Systems Biology, helping attendees stay updated with current research trends. We will explore various computational and experimental methods applied to biological systems, and how these techniques are advancing the understanding of complex biological networks.

Trends and Solutions in Systems Biology

ICASB-25 aims to bring attention to the current trends in Systems Biology, particularly in solving biological challenges. The conference will showcase advancements in synthetic biology, bioinformatics, and systems modeling, and highlight practical solutions to problems like drug development, disease modeling, and personalized medicine.

Interdisciplinary Collaborations

The conference seeks to foster interdisciplinary collaborations between biologists, bioinformaticians, engineers, and clinicians. It will explore how combining expertise from diverse fields can lead to breakthroughs in the understanding of biological systems and the development of new therapeutic approaches.

Conference Program

The conference program will feature keynote speeches, panel discussions, workshops, and poster sessions from renowned experts in the field. Attendees will have the opportunity to network, share ideas, and engage in thought-provoking discussions on the future of Systems Biology.
