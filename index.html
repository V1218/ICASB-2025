Thrust Areas

The thrust areas encompass cutting-edge research fields including Biosystematics, Integrative Biology, Genomics, and Proteomics. Focus is directed toward understanding biological systems, disease mechanisms, and the application of nanotechnology in agriculture and medicine. These domains also prioritize advancements in synthetic biology and big data analytics to address global challenges.

About Our Program (Venue)

The "International Conference on Advances in System Biology" will be hosted at our state-of-the-art venue, offering an inspiring environment for innovation and collaboration. Attendees will benefit from keynote lectures, parallel oral presentations, poster sessions, specialized workshop, and networking events. This conference provides an unparalleled opportunity to engage with leading experts and explore cutting-edge research in the field of system biology.
