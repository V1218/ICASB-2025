DBT, Govt of India Sponsored Two-Day Workshop

Workshop Announcement A DBT, Govt of India-sponsored two-day workshop titled “Essential Tools and Techniques in Molecular Biology” will be held on 7th and 8th March. Participants will receive hands-on training in advanced techniques such as PCR, RT-PCR, Sanger Sequencing, Cloning, Photosynthetic Analysis, Plant Tissue Culture, and Blotting Techniques.

  • Seats Available: Limited to 30 participants.
  • Workshop Fee: ₹500 (in addition to other charges).
