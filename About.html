About Our University

Dr. Hari Singh Gour University, formerly known as University of Saugor, is a Central University in the city of Sagar, the state of Madhya Pradesh, India. It was founded on 18 July 1946, during the British Raj, and is the oldest university in Madhya Pradesh.

Key Points

History and Foundation

Dr. Hari Singh Gour University, founded by Dr. Hari Singh Gour, a distinguished jurist, scholar, and philanthropist, on July 18, 1946, under the original name "University of Saugor." As the oldest university in Madhya Pradesh, it stands as a testament to Dr. Gour's vision for educational reform in central India. His personal contribution of savings to establish the institution was a groundbreaking gesture of philanthropy, reflecting his commitment to advancing education in the region. In recognition of its academic excellence and potential, the Government of India elevated the university to Central University status in 2009, enhancing its prestige, autonomy, and access to greater funding and resources, positioning it as a prominent hub for higher learning in the country.

Campus and Infrastructure

Nestled amidst the picturesque Patharia Hills in Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, the university spans an expansive 1318 acres, offering a serene and conducive environment for learning. The Nehru Library, one of central India's largest academic libraries, boasts over 3.2 lakh books, journals, e-resources, and rare collections, providing invaluable resources to students, faculty, and researchers. The campus is equipped with cutting-edge laboratories across various disciplines, fostering innovation and research in science, technology, and social sciences. In addition, the university offers separate hostels for male and female students, along with residential quarters for faculty and staff, creating a vibrant and supportive campus community. With excellent sports facilities for cricket, basketball, athletics, and more, along with gymnasiums and recreation centers, the campus ensures students have ample opportunities to stay active and engaged beyond academics.

Academic Programs

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya, located in Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, is a prestigious public university offering diverse academic programs at undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctorate levels. Spanning a sprawling 1,312.89-acre campus surrounded by forests and the Vindhya Range, the university features 11 schools that cater to disciplines like Applied Sciences, Business Management, Humanities, Law, Music, Pharmaceutical Sciences, Physical Sciences, and Social Sciences. It also offers specialized programs in Computer Applications, including BCA and MCA, and a wide range of engineering disciplines. Notable offerings include a four-year integrated B.Sc. B.Ed. program that equips students to teach at upper primary and secondary levels, along with various distance education options for UG, PG diplomas, and advanced research programs. Specializations in its B.Sc. courses include fields such as Botany, Zoology, Physics, Microbiology, Computer Science, and more, making DHGV a top academic institution in central India.

Research and Innovation

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya, Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, is committed to fostering research and innovation through several dedicated initiatives. The Sophisticated Instruments Centre (SIC) and the Center for Advanced Research (CAR) provide state-of-the-art facilities and high-end instruments for scientific research, supporting students, faculty, and external researchers, with CAR being linked to the Government of India's i-STEM scheme. The university’s Research and Development Directorate (DRD) facilitates various developmental activities and supports faculty in securing funding for major research projects while also funding select initiatives internally. DHGV emphasizes research in regional and local issues, focusing on sustainable development and areas like new drug delivery systems, nano-biotechnology, and herbal medicinal products. It provides financial assistance for faculty and scholars to participate in conferences, publish doctoral theses, and encourages the publication of research findings in quality journals. Additionally, the university actively promotes innovation by supporting faculty in filing patents.

Accreditations and Achievements

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya, Sagar, Madhya Pradesh, is a distinguished institution recognized for its academic excellence and research contributions. Re-accredited with an "A" grade by NAAC under the UGC, it was declared a Central University on January 15, 2009. The university has received certificates of participation from the Indian Institute of Remote Sensing, Dehradun (ISRO, Department of Space, GOI), and secured funding for various projects from UGC, DBT, DST, and MPCST. Notable achievements include multiple awards, such as Hemant Kumar Rawat’s Best Oral Presentation at the RACBS National Seminar, and Best Poster Awards at MRSC-2015, FDEOLS-2014, and other conferences. Shubham Nema also earned a Best Poster Award at the National Symposium on Frontiers in Modern Biology, showcasing the university’s focus on research excellence and innovation.

Student Life and Extracurricular Activities

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya Sagar, India, offers a vibrant range of extracurricular activities to foster students' holistic development. The university hosts numerous clubs and organizations, including music, dance, photography, fine arts, literary, and theater clubs, providing platforms for creative expression. Its extensive sports facilities include indoor and outdoor arenas, separate gyms for boys and girls, and large fields for games, with tournaments held at inter-university, inter-college, and inter-school levels. Cultural events like national and international conferences, seminars, and artistic activities such as tabla performances, photography exhibitions, classical and light singing, and skits add to the university's dynamic environment. A student council, established in 2018, further enhances student participation and leadership. These activities help students build social confidence, friendships, and essential soft skills like communication, teamwork, organization, and time management.

Community Engagement and Social Initiatives

The university maintains industry ties to offer students direct access to employers and career counseling for informed decisions. Placement opportunities span various fields, including engineering, management, and law. DHSGSU focuses on enhancing both technical and soft skills to prepare students for the competitive job market.

Vision and Mission

The vision of Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya is to be a center of excellence in higher education and research, promoting academic brilliance, innovation, and social responsibility to contribute to the overall development of students and society. Its mission is to provide quality education, foster research and innovation, and create an environment that nurtures intellectual, cultural, and social growth. The university is committed to developing skilled professionals with strong ethical values, empowering marginalized communities, and engaging in sustainable development initiatives to address regional and global challenges.

Future Plans and Development Goals

Dr. Harisingh Gour Vishwavidyalaya, is focused on becoming a leading center for academic excellence and research. The university plans to expand its infrastructure by enhancing laboratories, libraries, and digital learning platforms to support cutting-edge research and technology-driven education. It aims to establish more industry partnerships to provide students with greater internship and job placement opportunities. DHSGSU is committed to increasing its focus on interdisciplinary studies, fostering collaboration between departments, and introducing new courses in emerging fields like artificial intelligence, data science, and renewable energy. The university also plans to strengthen its community outreach programs, focusing on rural development and social responsibility, and aims to achieve international recognition by improving research output and global collaborations. Additionally, DHSGSU is working towards obtaining more research grants and funding to support its faculty and students in innovative projects and initiatives.
